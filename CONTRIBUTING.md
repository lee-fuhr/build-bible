# Contributing

This is a living methodology document. Contributions welcome:

- **Typos and clarity fixes** — PRs welcome, no issue needed
- **New patterns or anti-patterns** — open an issue first describing the pattern, its evidence, and where it fits
- **Corrections** — if something is wrong, open an issue with the specific section and what you've observed

The evolution protocol (section 8 of the bible) describes how changes are evaluated. The bar for adding a new principle is high — it needs cross-project evidence and adversarial review.
